Convert Hard English into Easy English using AI

Easy English AI is an AI-based application that converts difficult or literary English sentences into simple, easy-to-understand English. This project focuses on understanding the situation first, not just replacing words. The goal is to explain the meaning clearly, like a kind teacher explaining to a beginner.
